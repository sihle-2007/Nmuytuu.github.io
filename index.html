Changing Your Course

Interested in changing your course? You may qualify to transition from a Bachelor's program to a Diploma or Higher Certificate. Here's how you can apply:

  • Eligibility: Check if your current qualifications match the requirements for the course you wish to transfer to.
  • Application Process: Submit your application online with your updated qualifications and course details.
  • Important Dates: Make sure to apply before the deadline to ensure consideration for the next intake.

How to Apply for Accommodation

Living on campus is an essential part of the university experience. To apply for accommodation, follow these steps:

  1. Visit the accommodation portal and create an account.
  2. Fill in the online application form with your details and preferred accommodation type.
  3. Submit all required documents, including proof of registration.
  4. Wait for confirmation of your accommodation assignment.
